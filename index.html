Software is a solution for people

A web application or a mobile application design has the user’s needs as the first priority.

The process

Experience

Your target users need to find the software easy to use, and naturally focus on what you’re providing.
So, we at Splense make sure to understand your target users before building the user story and experience.
This means, we make it simple and intuitive.

Interface

Your users need to find what you are providing without being distracted.
So, we design an interface which blends into the background and at the same time, enhances the visuals of the software.

Development

It’s your software, you will need to know the how, what, why and when of the development.
So, the development is a transparent process, with constant feedback from you. We don’t believe in hierarchy at Splense.
Your queries are answered instantly with just a call to anyone here.

Support

Our relationship does not end with development, in fact, we love support at Splense.
We see it as having your unique identity imprint itself on the solution.
After all, it is your solution.
